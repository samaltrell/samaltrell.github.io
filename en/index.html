Lair is a board game club at the Faculty of Engineering LTH. The club is open to everyone; you do not have to be a student to play at our gaming events.

We have regular meetups every Thursday, starting at 18.00 in E-huset's basement. We meet at the study rooms in the south corridor next to E:Falk. (See the map.)

Lair has a board game library in our cabinets in the basement, but everyone is welcome to bring their own games to the club.

You need a student ID card to get intothe building, since the doors lock at 17.00. If you want to be let in, write in the discord and we will open for you.

Contact: info@lair.se
